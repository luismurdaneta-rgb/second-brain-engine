Project Falcon — Kickoff Brief

Falcon is a pilot to migrate the Reporting Service off the legacy Monolith and
onto an event-driven pipeline. Primary stakeholders are the Platform Team and
the Data Team. Target milestone is a working prototype by the end of Q3.

Open questions:
- Do we use Kafka or a managed queue for the Event Bus?
- Who owns the Migration Runbook?

Next steps: schedule a design review, draft the Architecture Decision Record.
